ORDER

Entitlement to service connection for an acquired psychiatric disorder, to include schizophrenia, is granted.

FINDING OF FACT

Affording the Veteran with the benefit of the doubt, his schizophrenia began during active service.

CONCLUSION OF LAW

The criteria for entitlement to service connection for an acquired psychiatric disorder, to include schizophrenia, have been met. 38 U.S.C. §§ 1110, 5107(b); 38 C.F.R. §§ 3.303, 3.304, 4.125.

REASONS AND BASES FOR FINDING AND CONCLUSION

The Veteran had active service from March 1999 to July 1999. This case comes before the Board of Veterans' Appeals (Board) on appeal from a September 2013 rating decision by the Department of Veterans Affairs (VA) Regional Office (RO) in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
